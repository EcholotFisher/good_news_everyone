NEW YORK, Sept. 23, 2015 /PRNewswire-iReach/ -- New rankings from NewsWhip , of the largest brand publishers on social media in August 2015, sees Red Bull move up the rankings to claim No1. 
Photo - http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20150923/269797 
After months in 2nd or 3rd and threatening to take the top spot, master content marketer, Red Bull has rightly claimed the top step on the podium. Microsoft retains its podium slot, with Playstation newly entering the top three. 
Notable insights and trends in the report, include: -- Ikea storming into the top 25, taking 4th place -- Ferrari powering into the top 10 for the first time, grabbing 9th -- Ben and Jerry's return to the top 10, after their success in June -- ICICI Bank setting a NewsWhip record for engagement per article -- Facebook continuing to be the dominant social platform for brand publishers, excepting Cisco, with its focus on LinkedIn 
Commenting on the rankings, NewsWhip Marketing Director, Will Bancroft, has this to say: 
"Our monthly rankings on brand publishers on social continue to show which brands are most effectively executing global scale content marketing, and which content types and assets are powering this performance. Congratulations to Red Bull - a worthy No1 this month - whose content is packed full of high fidelity images, video and brilliant story telling!"
